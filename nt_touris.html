Introduction Nha Trang tourism

Tourism Nha Trang - Khanh Hoa Province is a province in the South Central Coast region, an area of natural land area of 5205 km2, is the large island with more than 200 large and small islands. Khanh Hoa has abundant labor resources and a population of 1,137,792 million people (2006), is one of the country's 10 provinces with large number of intellectuals. The province currently has 40 research units and more than 20,500 technology staff have college degree, undergraduate and more than 200 qualified staff in the university. The rate of trained labor over 25%...
Khanh Hoa has three key economic development areas: South of Cam Ranh Bay is a very important position on defense and security and economic development. Cam Ranh Airport is located in the center of Cam Ranh peninsula, is one of the few airports with runways big and long in Vietnam today, the upcoming construction investment will be upgraded to become the international airport. Ba Ngoi port simultaneously after upgrading and expansion is one of the important ports in the port system of the South Central region, create favorable conditions for the development of trade between Khanh Hoa with other parts of the country and international.

North Bay office is located in the easternmost geographical coordinates of Vietnam, international waters 14 km, near the junction of international maritime routes. Van Phong is a large bay with 41,000 ha of water surface, with a water depth of 20-30 m, relatively airtight. Conditions and potential, the government has planned to build in this area and the International Container Transshipment Port multidisciplinary integrated economic area, including: commercial, industrial, tourism ...

Cảng trung chuyển Container Quốc tế Vân Phong, liên kết thuận lợi với đường bộ, đường sắt, hàng không, kín gió, an toàn, có đủ khả năng để có thể cạnh tranh với các cảng trung chuyển Container đang hoạt động ở khu vực như: Singapo, Hồng Công, Kaohsiung…Tiềm năng phát triển cảng trung chuyển Container Quốc tế Vân Phong có thể đạt tới 17,5-17,8 triệu TEU/năm. Bên cạnh đó, Vân phong có khí hậu tương đối ôn hoà, cảnh quan môi trường đẹp là nơi có tiềm năng để phát triển du lịch sinh thái, là nơi có điều kiện lý tưởng để phát triển kinh tế thủy sản…

In the center of Nha Trang Bay is recognized as one of the most beautiful bays in the world. Nha Trang with favorable natural conditions both in terms of location, landscape, climate, together with a background in history, humanities, Nha Trang - Khanh Hoa able to develop various kinds of tourism .
