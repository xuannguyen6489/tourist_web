Da Nang Cham Joint Institute

The Cham Institute is located at No. 02, September 2 Da Nang city. Located at the intersection of Trung Nu Vuong and Bach Dang. 1892 Minister of Quang Nam is Charles Lemire has collected artifacts Champa sculpture scattered in Tra Kieu, USA Jiang ... on display at the city park located on a low hill beside the temple Long Player. After Charles Lemire Camille de Paris has collected a number of artifacts Champa sculpture in Le, Bang An ... and a few other places. First, the museum is a rectangular structure (sequences between Galery today) completed in May 1916. 1935 demand growth museum building 2 more blocks.
Currently the museum displays approximately 300 original works of sandstone and a few made of earthenware. All dating continuous evolution of Champa art from the 7th to 15th centuries.
The museum is the only place in Vietnam and around the world to study the Champa culture has lost a remarkable level of development of culture and art. Content on display in the museum include sculptures such as altars, statues, statue, dancers, relief, spiritual objects, animals, etc. All have shown the evolution of the art conditions Champa engraved from early to late period, help for tourists as well as researchers have a fairly complete view of the stage of development of the art of Champa over time.

Layout of the museum's galleries Champa sculpture from right to left as follows:
1. Galery My Son (works from the 8th century - 11th century)
2. Galery Quang Tri (7 to 8 century)
3. Quang Nam Galery (7,8,9,10,11 century)
4. Tra Kieu Galery (7,8,9,10 century)
5. Galery Quang Ngai (Century 9 - 11)
6. Galery Kontum (Century 14 - 15)
7. Galery Dong Duong (9th century)
8. Galery Quang Binh (7th century - 8)
9. Binh Dinh Galery (12,13,14 Century)
10. Galery Thap Mam (Century 12 - 13)
Cham Museum is a creation based on the motifs of ancient Cham art, architecture is a small but simple and unique.
