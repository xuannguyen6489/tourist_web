Non Nuoc-Ngu Hanh Son

Mountain resort with beautiful beaches located adjacent to the Ngu Hanh Son, Da Nang 8 km to the southeast. This is tourism hyphen between Marble Mountain and three world cultural heritage: the ancient town of Hoi An, My Son and Hue.
Non Nuoc beach stretching 5 km as an arc of white sand, gentle slope, blue water, sunny and windy. Environment here is fresh, the ideal temperature, less influenced by the northeast monsoon, as a condition for visitors year-round resort
Mountain Beach in Da Nang, Forbes, America's leading magazine selected as one of the most attractive beaches and the planet in 2005. It is now Sandy Beach Resort construction and management.
Mountain resort with beautiful beaches located adjacent to the Ngu Hanh Son, Da Nang 8 km to the southeast. This is tourism hyphen between Marble Mountain and three world cultural heritage: the ancient town of Hoi An, My Son and Hue. Along the beach, the white sand forest is lush casuarinas years old, cool, Outing wind. This is a place of rest, camping, picnic ideal. After the expedition and have fun, you can relax in the sauna therapy, jacuzzi and sauna. When night falls, the moon, you can drop her on the couch coastal quiet, listening to the waves and enjoy the melodic magic
Features of Non Nuoc Beach is to get the signal from the qualities, climate, weather, salinity ... very consistent with the kind of sea sports, especially skiing waves (surfing). In 1993, there took place the international surfing contests with the participation of nearly 40 athletes from many countries around the world.

Non Nuoc Beach also has many features fresh seafood such as squid, shrimp, crabs, snails ... so that you enjoy; rare algae such as seaweed gold, seaweed spinach have high export value.
Mountain Beach just 5 minutes walk you can visit and admire the Ngu Hanh Son (also known as the Marble Mountain) has been classified by the State historical country in 1980.
Marble with populations of 5 majestic granite mountains are named: Kim Son, Moc Son, Thuy Son, Vulcan painter represents the five elements of the universe (according to the Five Elements theory). In the mountains there are many ancient temples, more serious deep caves, mysterious..
