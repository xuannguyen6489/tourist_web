Vacation Package

Quang Binh - Hue Tour

price: $499

Buy Ticket
(5 days 4 nights, departing on every Thursday or Sunday weekly.)


Day 01: Welcome - TOUR Da Nang - Hoi An (lunch & dinner)
Lunch: Pick up at the airport / station Da Nang to lunch with famous specialty of Da Nang: Rice Paper pork 2 skin. Depart for Hoi An, check K / hotel to rest.
Afternoon: Hundred of enjoying the beauty of Hoi An Ancient Town (World Cultural Heritage), brilliantly reflected the Hoai River, once a busy commercial port of the second century the Cham and Vietnamese from the sixteenth century . Hoi An's Cua Dai beach. Evening: Dinner at restaurant. Join Old Town night shimmering with city view the fourth centuries ago.

Day 02: Hoi An - BA NA (Breakfast Lunch & Dinner)
Morning: Breakfast. Leaving Hoi An to Da Nang, visit the heritage and the Marble Mountains and Non Nuoc stone carving village. Noon: Lunch restaurant.
Afternoon: you go Bana, transit vehicles onto the Peak, visit the resorts Bana Bynight, Linh Ung Pagoda Bana .... get a hotel room
Evening: Dinner at restaurant. Join campfire program (if any)

DAY 03: BA NA - Hue (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)
Morning: Breakfast. Suspension Bridge Tour, God conquered the mountain with an altitude of 1420m above sea level, go cable Treo Bana. Pick up you down the mountain, in the car Lang
Noon: Lunch restaurant in Lang Co
Afternoon: Visit the Citadel (Royal Palace of the 13th king of the Nguyen Dynasty, the last feudal dynasty in Vietnam: Ngo Mon Gate, Thai Hoa Palace, Forbidden City, The Temple, Hien Lam, Cuu Dinh) and Thien ancient Mu.
Dinner: Check in at the restaurant. Dragon boat heard CA HUE and flower drop for good luck on the Perfume.

DAY 04: HUE - Phong Nha - Hue (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)
Morning: Breakfast early. Leave Hue Phong Nha - Ke Bang National Park - a World Natural Heritage. Visit the Holy Land La Vang (awarded State basilica) and photographed Latitude 17 - Hien Luong Bridge - Ben Hai River (About temporary line separating two South - North for 20 years, from 1954 to 1973 ).
Noon: Lunch restaurant in Phong Nha.
Afternoon: Sitting boat against the Son River to conquer the Phong Nha: Top punished, Hang Tien & Imperial deep mountains where rivers groundwater from Lao flow to, admire the beautiful stalactites blocks are tectonic in nature . Depart Hue along the Truong Son - Legendary Ho Chi Minh City. Tours and incense heroic martyrs in the Truong Son Martyrs Cemetery. Evening: Dinner and sleep in K / restaurant.

DAY 05: HUE - off (Breakfast, Lunch & Dinner)
Morning: Breakfast. Visiting the Tomb of Khai Dinh Tomb of the 13th dynasty king with unique architecture combines the East-West cultural and specialty shopping Dong Ba market.
Noon: Lunch restaurant.
Afternoon: Drop the airport / Ga Hue / Danang. End of tour
