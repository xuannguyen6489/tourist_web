VISIT THE TOWER SHE PONAGAR

Nha Trang City, tourists can not visit Tower Ms. Ponagar - a relic of the famous historical and cultural city. Tower Ms. Ponagar is a population pyramid with the unique architecture of the Cham people and is a work of art represents the architecture and sculpture of the Cham people have existed in the 10th century.
Temple Champa kings is Harivacman building in the 813 - 817 to commemorate the Mother country called Ponugar or Ponagar. Worship God Y Marian An Na (Ponagar) shows the diversity of the Cham cultural fusion - Vietnam and Cham - India.If worship God Ya Na Hon Chen (Hue) is a combination of with Dao Tam Phu form of the Vietnamese, the Thien Yana (or Ponagar) in Thap Ba Nha Trang is a combination Thien Yana and beliefsBrahmanism (An). Thien Yana become goddess-wife of Civa, symbolizing beauty and dance. In the tower tourists can find caught the object is being god Ganesa, the god of war Karhykey ... bearing the Brahmanical religion.

Relic Temple consists of four towers, two temples and a holiday home, which has two towers, a 18 m high tower worship Civa and a 22.48 m high main tower, church goddess Ponagar. All the towers are built of brick with terracotta quite delicate and perfect technique. Largest tower has 4 floors, each floor has its false door decorated quite elaborately.


Here, guests will discover Cham sculpture carved elaborately. Gate is the Statue of Durga dancing between the two musicians. On top of the tower with a statue of Shiva riding a bull god Nandin. Many tourists be surprised to see the "big picture" dancer, boating ... are carved into the stone statues on the outside of the tower. Tourists and locals to visit and worship at the Temple east.
