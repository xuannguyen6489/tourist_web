VISIT HON NGOC VIET

Hon Ngoc Viet located in Hon Tre, is a five-star hotel in the beautiful valley, one of the nine most beautiful bays in the world. Once visitors to Nha Trang arrive islands Hon Tre will never forget the beautiful moments of relaxation in international-class resort Vinpearl Land. This is the ideal place for tourists with beautiful stretches of white sand, the bright sunshine all year round shining down like jade green waters. Resorts Sofitel VinPearl is a hotel complex situated on Hon Tre island consists of 500 rooms 5 star standard, including 35 suites and 4 rooms heads of state gia.Vinpearl Land is an entertainment resort 5 star island resort and world class in Vietnam.
Here visitors can get to see the outdoor art shows also the music venues, great art, sound, superb light and modern. Complete outdoor landscape scene very romantic and majestic middle of a valley close to the sea, four surface as mountains ... Visitors are able to see musical performances and participate in a lot of games here .
