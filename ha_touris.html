Introduction Hoi An tourism

Hoi An Tourism - Hoi An, on every occasion in March or in the summer, visitors will feel first thing in Hoi An is the sun. Sun shine on the roof moss, the sun sparkling on the foliage green, golden dense on the winding old streets ... Although a tourist destination, and the houses in the old town are trade advantage, but the old walls painted yellow and the brown doorway rigs lined paper flowers of all colors still making Hoi An city the most calm I've ever come across
Goods sold here also no Deleting as a few other destinations. Dresses, bags, shoes colorful silk, natural stone jewelry, crafts and fine art, and pictures, map carved enough material, both books again, are furnished very moderate in the store makes buyers feel very comfortable but not glitzy.
Engrossed in the store, until the afternoon light has to Hoai River. The sun about to set in gold river sparkling hairy. Step very lightly on the old bridge, feeling the spirit of Hoi An is absorbed in his own, very gentle but extremely deep.

Drifted into a small alley, stopped in front of an old gate donut table. Have heard much about the cauldron cakes, high floor, fried wonton or chicken rice famous Hoi An specialties delicious, but it was interesting to sit inside the house floor ancient dark warm brown wood truss columns, overlooking the yard small fern grows full and the sky fell on the first afternoon, tasting enough delicious cake wrapped in foil packages, talk to the sales lady, bent a shot bending and hair were white smile still fresh as if still still the glorious young son. As night began to let down Hoi start up of the lights. The tops enough blue gold purple red lantern hanging in the store, on the porch in the city, filled glare in the trees and shimmering on the water tender Huai River shines brightly makes Hoi An and suspension virtual strange.

In the ancient house was requisitioned as cafes or restaurants, restaurant style full cage lights are lit bright, light brown blend with the shadow of the roof and furniture to create aold definition very own, as if I had strayed into a different era, a different space. Go on the road along the river bank watching the lanterns, hear the wind blowing from the river Outing, you'll feel your heart so sweet and mellow to the strange ...
