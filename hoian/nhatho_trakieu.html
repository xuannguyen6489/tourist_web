TRA KIEU CHURCH

Located in the village of Tra Kieu Duy Son Duy Xuyen district of Da Nang City 37km southwest. From fork Nam Phuoc on National Highway 1A through Duy Xuyen district under the table just the way you go to the My Son Tra Kieu is about 7km to the church.

Built in 1772 in a different location and then moved to the position as it is today in 1865. In 1971 Father Peter Le Nhu Hao space between the current cathedral building in 17th-century European architecture. Next to the church is the monastery (built in 1867), the traditional exhibitors and the Church of Our Lady was built in 1898 on a 60 meter high hill, surrounded by gardens and trees all year round shade shine, as do add the beauty is both ancient and peaceful church. Standing here you can enjoy the cool fresh air and panoramic around watching the scene of a large residential area, weaving between blue border specifically of trees along the banks of the river Thu Bon dream

In the church there is a well which the parish is very sacred holy wells, taking in all diseases are eliminated, the body becomes healthy. If you have a chance to come here you do not miss the opportunity to test this. Tra Kieu Church is not just a place to lay around the belief that every year, at Christmas, in the chapel of Tra Kieu often organized large-scale ceremony attracted the participation of many parishioners come from all over the country.

At this point you can take photos or memories can also visit the souvenir shop choose certain items as a gift. Especially at the souvenir booth next to the church host tourists to see these precious artifacts found around the church Tra Kieu. Besides can combine visit Tra Kieu capital, the neighboring villages such as: textile East Yen-Ban Thach Thi Lai projector.
